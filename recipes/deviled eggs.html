deviled eggs

description

These deviled eggs are delicious for Easter brunch and are always popular at holiday parties

ingredients

  • six boiled eggs
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 onion
  • 1 celery
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 paprika

steps

  1. Cut the boiled eggs in half and remove the yolks. Reserve the whites.
  2. Mash the yolks with a fork and add the remaining ingredients.
  3. Stuff or pipe the filling into the egg white halves.
