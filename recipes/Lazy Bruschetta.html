Lazy Bruschetta

DESCRIPTION

Tomatoes and cheese are piled onto slices of bread and broiled. Easy bruschetta that even tomato haters will love.

ingredients

  • 4 slices Italian bread
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded cheese blend

steps

  1. Preheat your oven's broiler.
  2. Arrange the slices of bread in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Mix together the olive oil and garlic powder in a cup or small bowl. Brush onto the slices of bread. Cover each piece of bread with tomato slices, then sprinkle with as much cheese as you like. Drizzle just a bit of the olive oil over the cheese
  4. Broil for 3 to 5 minutes, until cheese is melted and bread is toasted. Make sure you keep an eye on it - it doesn't take long
